What is bargain.com?

This is an app that provides customers with a platform where they can bargain for a product or service they need, without having to physically move around town looking for businesses willing to offer them the product or service they need at the price they need them at.

How does bargain.com work?

If you are a customer willing to bargain for a product or service you just have to upload the product or service with your bargain price, in our platform.

Businesses in our platform goes through the uploaded products and if any of them feels they can offer you the product or service you want, at the price you have posted, they will text you and you can both talk.
